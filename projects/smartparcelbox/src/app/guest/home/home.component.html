Key Features – Smart Online Shopping

Take a complete control of your online shopping with some of these key features of the Shopping Manager app by the Smart Parcel Box magna.

Maintain Orders Detail

Record all details of your shopping in one place. Connect store orders are auto imported, but you could input and keep detailed record of all your shopping from all stores.

Give Delivery Instructions

Just one click instructs the courier and your retailer to deliver to you Smart Parcel Box. They get instructions, location and picture of your Smart Parcel Box

Live courier tracking

Monitor live status of your package from most popular couriers. No more depending on mails or notifications from separate retailers and couriers

Manage Order Status

The orders status is clearly displayed and can be managed by the users. The Orders can be searched and also be sorted on the basis of their status.

Track Returns & Refunds

Scan the return package shipping label and get live tracking information. This allows you to chase retailers for refund with a one click refund mails which can be personalised by you.

Share Access with Family

Keep orders of the whole family together in one place and track their deliveries in one place. No more looking for details on various phones, laptops and computers.

Efficient Communication

Save time by sending mails to the retailers or couriers with all order details using your favourite mailing service on your phone with just one click

Manage Smart Parcel Box

Your Smart Parcel Box can now be registered on the App to help couriers know its location. Details can be edited and the app helps to notify couriers too

On All your Devices

Convenience of managing of your online shopping on your phone, tablet and desktop with ease. This app helps you to take complete control of your online shopping
