Why Should I register my Smart Parcel Box?

The Shopping Manager App (developed by the Smart Parcel Box) helps to communicate with the retailer and the courier to instruct them to drop the parcels to your Smart Parcel Box. It further reminds them that scanning or taking picture of your unique barcode will act as a proof of delivery and replaces the need of your signatures. By registering yours and by using the app, you can leave this instruction with much ease.

Each Smart Parcel Box is supplied with and identified by a unique barcode. Registering your box helps to assign a specific geographic coordinate to your parcel box. This means that your unique Smart Parcel Box barcode is an identifier of your address.

This geographical coordinate linked unique code is designed to be used by postal workers and courier companies to ascertain that the delivery has been made to the correct address.

By registering the Smart Parcel Box, the owner of the Smart Parcel Box also indemnifies the courier company from handing over the package personally to an individual at the address and authorises them to deliver the parcel to the Smart Parcel Box instead.

Some delivery services may require your signature for special signed for deliveries, we would still recommend registration of your Smart Parcel Box as more and more retailers and couriers are moving towards a contact free delivery and would like to have an alternative safe place to deliver your package and an alternative way to replace your signatures.

Also, should you need to replace the barcode or change your address for any reason, you can easily make these changes if your parcel box has been registered by the original owner.
