Why Should you register your Smart Parcel Box?

This app helps you to send instruction to the courier to deliver your package to the smart parcel box. It also authorises couriers and indemnifies them from handing over the package personally.

Its Unique barcode and location information differentiates each parcel box and the couriers may use them as an alternative to signatures for proof of delivery.
