How Genericpedia Works

1. Get medicine prescription

Ask your doctor to write the generic name along with the brand name on your prescription.
2. Search on Genericpedia

Search on Genericpedia with brand name or generic name to find best quality medicine at best cost.
3. Find and visit

Find the location of the nearest pharmacy on genericpedia and visit to buy the medicine.
